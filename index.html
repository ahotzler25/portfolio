Greetings, Traveller!

I'm a game writer and narrative designer that loves to craft immersive stories to support emotional, fun, and rivetting gameplay. I specialize in narrative, storytelling, dialogue, style, characterization, and rhetoric, but am also knowledgeable in narrative structures, narrative design, and quest design.

I hold a Master of Arts, English with an emphasis in Creative Writing. My thesis was a branching narrative, scripted and written in Twine 2.x using Sugarcube 2 titled "Lost in the Shadows Between Worlds" (Link to project on itch.io).

I also participated in a GameDev.Tv's 2023 game jam using the theme "two dimensions". "The Adventure of Ignatius" (Link to itch.io) is a quirky 3D puzzle/platformer I co-created in Unreal Engine 5 using Blueprints. My teammate was primarily responsible for the visual scripting and menu design, while my primary responsibilities were level design, puzzle design, audio implementation, and UX.

I also programming and scripting experience, be it Twine (primarily SugarCube 2), Python, web development and tools (HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, React), or C++ (certificate from GameDev.TV: "C++ Fundamentals: Game Programming for Beginners").

I have over two years professional quality assurance experience, one year as a QA Tester II/III on an unreleased AAA game, and just over one year as a QA Analyst I assisting in launching seasons 6 and 7 in Halo: Infinite and released Ara: History Untold. I'm familiar with the entire SDLC, and thanks to my background in both QA and programming, I'm comfortable collaborating and brainstorming with all game dev teams.
